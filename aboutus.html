What is eDorf?

eDorf is an American multinational e-commerce corporation based in Garden City, New York that facilitates consumer-to-consumer and business-to-consumer sales through its website. eDorf was founded by Jake, Mike, and Mollie in the spring of 2019, and became a notable success story of the dot-com bubble. eDorf is a multibillion-dollar business with operations in several countries, as of 2019. The company manages the eDorf website, an online auction and shopping website in which people and businesses buy and sell a wide variety of goods and services worldwide. The website is free to use for buyers, but sellers are charged fees for listing items after a limited number of free listings, and again when those items are sold.
